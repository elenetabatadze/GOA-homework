Jupiter

Jupiter is one of the largest planets in our solar system.

About Jupiter

Jupiter is a gas giant in our solar system, and it is enormous. Its diameter is about 143,000 km, which is larger than the combined diameter of all other planets. It has a diameter 11.2 times that of Earth.

Jupiter's atmosphere is primarily composed of helium and hydrogen. The planet is also famous for its Great Red Spot, a massive storm that has been raging for centuries.
